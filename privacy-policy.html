Introduction

Welcome to Home Reno Hotspot. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our service to connect with home renovation contractors in the New York City area.

By submitting your information through our service, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

What Information We Collect

When you use Home Reno Hotspot, we collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Project Details: Information about your home renovation project, including project type, location, timeline, and any specific requirements
  • Property Information: Your property address and details about the areas you want to renovate
  • Communication Records: Messages and conversations you have through our platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Contractor Matching: To match you with qualified contractors in your area who can help with your renovation project
  • Communication: To send you SMS updates about your project, contractor matches, and appointment scheduling
  • Service Coordination: To coordinate appointments and facilitate communication between you and contractors
  • Service Improvement: To improve our matching algorithm and enhance our service quality
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions and provide assistance

SMS Communications & Consent

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Home Reno Hotspot. These messages may include:

  • Confirmation of your project submission
  • Contractor match notifications
  • Appointment reminders and scheduling updates
  • Service-related communications

Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. You may also reply HELP for assistance.

How We Share Your Information

With Contractors

We share your contact information and project details with contractors who match your renovation needs. This allows them to reach out to you to discuss your project and provide quotes.

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform, such as SMS delivery services and data storage providers. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for us to use your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
