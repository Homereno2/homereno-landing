When homeowners consent to receive SMS

Homeowners consent to receive SMS from Home Reno Hotspot in two ways:

  • Facebook Lead Ad form. When they submit a Facebook Lead Ad form, the form includes an SMS consent checkbox and/or consent language. By submitting the form, they agree to receive SMS about their renovation project.
  • Texting us first. When they text us first (initiate the conversation), they give implied consent by choosing to communicate with us via SMS. We use that channel to respond and support their project.

Exact consent language shown on Facebook Lead Ads

The following language is shown on our Facebook Lead Ad forms:

By submitting this form, you agree to receive SMS messages from Home Reno Hotspot regarding your renovation project. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Message and data rates may apply.

How homeowners can opt out

Homeowners can opt out of SMS at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from Home Reno Hotspot. After STOP, they will receive a final confirmation and no further messages.
  • Emailing help@homerenohotspot.com to request removal from SMS communications.

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
