What is Home Reno Hotspot?

Home Reno Hotspot connects NYC homeowners with vetted contractors for renovation and repair projects. We help you get qualified leads, schedule calls, and decide which jobs to take—all in one place.

How it works

  1. Submit your request — Tell us about your project (e.g. kitchen remodel, bathroom repair) and when you’re available.
  2. We qualify — We confirm your project details and timeline so contractors see only relevant leads.
  3. We match you — Vetted contractors in your area get your lead and can accept or pass.
  4. You decide — You get introduced to the contractor and choose whether to move forward. No obligation.

SMS messages

Message frequency: Message frequency varies. You may receive texts when we have updates about your request or when a contractor is matched to your project.

Rates: Msg & data rates may apply.

STOP: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS. You will receive a confirmation message.

HELP: Reply HELP for assistance or visit this page anytime.
