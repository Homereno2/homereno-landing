For Contractors

Join our network of vetted renovation pros in the NYC area.

Home Reno Hotspot connects homeowners with contractors for painting, kitchen and bath work, flooring, and other renovation projects. If you’re a licensed or qualified contractor serving the New York City metro area and want qualified leads and scheduled call windows (not cold calls), we’d like to hear from you.

What We Offer

  • Qualified leads — Homeowners are pre-qualified for project type, area, and timeline.
  • Scheduled introductions — You receive a booked call window so you know when to expect the homeowner.
  • Clear expectations — Project scope and area are shared so you can prepare and respond.
  • No bidding wars — One contractor is assigned per job; you’re not competing in an open auction.

What We Look For

We work with contractors who are legitimate, responsive, and able to serve NYC-area homeowners. We typically expect appropriate licensing (where required for your trade), insurance, and a professional approach to communication and scheduling.

How to Join

We’re currently onboarding contractors by referral and application. If you’re interested in joining the Home Reno Hotspot network, please reach out with your business name, trade(s), service area (boroughs or neighborhoods), and contact information.
