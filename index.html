Home Reno Hotspot

Connect with vetted renovation pros in NYC — fast, simple, and fair.

Home Reno Hotspot is an AI-driven platform that matches homeowners with qualified contractors for painting, kitchen and bath work, flooring, and other renovation projects in the New York City area. We handle the conversation, qualification, and scheduling so you get a serious pro on the phone — no bidding wars, no spam.

How it works

Share your project and timeline with us via text or our lead form. We qualify your request, book a call window, and connect you with a vetted contractor in your area. You decide whether to move forward — no obligation.
