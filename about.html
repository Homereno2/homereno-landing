About Home Reno Hotspot

Connecting NYC homeowners with vetted renovation professionals.

Home Reno Hotspot is a New York City–focused service that helps homeowners find qualified contractors for painting, kitchen and bath renovations, flooring, and other home improvement projects. We use technology and a clear process to match you with pros who are available, licensed where required, and ready to discuss your project.

Our Mission

We aim to make finding a renovation contractor straightforward and transparent. Instead of posting on generic boards or guessing who to call, you share your project once. We qualify your request, confirm your timeline, and introduce you to a contractor who fits—so you can focus on your project, not on endless back-and-forth.

How It Works

  • You tell us what you need. Share your project type, location, and timeline via our lead form or by texting us.
  • We qualify and schedule. We confirm details and book a call window that works for you.
  • We connect you with a pro. A vetted contractor in your area is assigned and will reach out during the scheduled window.
  • You decide. There’s no obligation. You talk to the contractor and decide whether to move forward.

Service Area

We currently serve the New York City metro area, including the five boroughs and nearby regions. Our contractor network is organized by area and project type so we can match you with someone who actually serves your neighborhood.
